Oral History Amplified

Amplify is the State Library of NSW interface for crowd-sourcing corrections to transcripts of oral history recordings. This site takes completed transcripts from Amplify and uses Natural Language Processing techniques to attempt to add value to them: topic segmentation and entity extraction.
